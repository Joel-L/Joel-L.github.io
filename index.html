Kriya Yoga

Many people have read Paramahamsa Yogananda's Autobiography of a Yogi and therefore heard of Kriya Yoga and its associated lineage. I was one of those people, and began to do some research into the method and its founders. Through this search, I met Narayana Baba, who eventually led me to Gurudev. (See LINEAGE page)
